## How credits work

RapportAPI uses simple credit packs — no subscriptions. Each research lookup costs **1 credit**. Buy credits in bulk for a better per-report price.

## Free credits

New accounts get **3 free credits** to try the API — no payment required.

## Credit lifecycle

- **Added** when you purchase a credit pack
- **Deducted** when you submit a research request (1 credit per lookup)
- **Refunded** automatically if a research request fails
- **No expiration** — credits never expire and don't reset

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance is visible in the [dashboard](https://rapportapi.com/home). The API returns a `402` error when you're out of credits:

```json
{
  "detail": "Insufficient credits. Please purchase more at rapportapi.com.",
  "status_code": 402
}
```

## Failed requests

If a research request fails after credits are deducted, we automatically refund the credit to your account. You'll see this as a `refund` transaction in your usage history.
